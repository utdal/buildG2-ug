    Account Request

    Who Can Get an Account?

    G2 accounts are available to UT Dallas faculty, staff, and students engaged in research computing. Access is typically granted through a PI (Principal Investigator) who owns or has access to a condo partition, or through shared access to the cpu-preempt and gpu-preempt partitions.

    How to Request an Account

    1. Visit the HPC Services page at hpc.utdallas.edu/services
    2. Locate Ganymede 2 under the list of systems
    3. Click the link that most closely matches your request
    4. If unsure, email circ-assist@utdallas.edu

    What You Will Receive

    Once your account is approved, you will receive:

    • A NetID-based username for the cluster
    • Access to the login nodes (g2-l-01, g2-l-02)
    • A home directory (~) with a 50 GB quota
    • Access to scratch space (~/scratch) with a 30 TB soft limit
    • Access to shared partitions: cpu-preempt and gpu-preempt
    • Access to your PI's condo partition (if applicable)

    Condo Access

    G2 is organized into condos — dedicated hardware owned by individual research groups. If your PI owns a condo:

    • You will be added to the condo's group and can submit jobs to that condo's partition
    • Condo jobs have priority over preemptive jobs and are not subject to preemption

    If you do not belong to a condo, you can still submit jobs to cpu-preempt and gpu-preempt, which provide access to all cluster nodes on a preemptive, first-come first-served basis.

    Need Help?

    • Email: circ-assist@utdallas.edu
    • HPC Services: hpc.utdallas.edu/services
